mintCat Data Deletion Request

mintCat does not require users to create an account.

Most user-entered budgeting data, such as income, expenses, subscriptions, wallets, and savings goals, is stored locally on the user's device. This data can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data from the device settings.

If you have made an in-app purchase or subscription, some purchase-related data may be processed by Google Play and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and verify purchases.

How to request data deletion

To request deletion of any data associated with mintCat, please contact us at:

Email: YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL_HERE

Please include the subject line: Data Deletion Request - mintCat

Data that may be deleted

We will delete or anonymize data associated with your request where possible, including data that may be stored or processed by our service providers for app functionality, purchase verification, and subscription management.

Data retention

Some purchase, transaction, or legal records may be retained where required by law, fraud prevention, tax, accounting, or platform policy requirements.
